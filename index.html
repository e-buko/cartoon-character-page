Beast

About Beast

Beast is the prince of a castle in a small village in France. After a selfish act toward a poor beggar, who turned out to be a sorceress, he was turned into a beast. The spell would only be lifted if someone fell in love with him.

If the final petal fell from the rose before someone grew to love Beast, he would remain a beast forever

Beast's Characteristics

  1. He is intimidating at first, but kind once you get to know him
  2. He hasn't spent time with anyone outside of his castle staff (also affected by the sorceress's spell) since he was turned into a beast
  3. He is pretty stubborn, but he begins to soften when he starts to fall in love with Belle
